[size=4][color=#00ffff]OVERVIEW[/color][/size]
Small SKSE plugin that automatically spins the inventory item preview (including the zoomed in version).
Manually spinning the preview takes precedence, dragging and letting go continues the rotation (see first two gifs).


[size=4][color=#00ffff]COMPATIBILITY[/color][/size]
Should be compatible with anything that doesn't also mess with the inventory item preview.
This started as a joke, but if you actually use/like it and run into issues, let me know and I'll look into it.


[size=4][color=#00ffff]CONFIGURATION[/color][/size]
[quote]Data\SKSE\Plugins\ItemPreviewAutoSpinning.ini[/quote]

[list]
[*][b]Auto-spin speed[/b]: Controls the automatic preview spin. Negative values reverse the direction.[/*]
[*][b]Resume delay[/b]: Controls how long auto-spin waits after you manually rotate the preview.[/*]
[*][b]Release spin[/b]: Keeps the preview spinning briefly after you drag and let go.[/*]
[*][b]Release spin duration[/b]: Controls how long the release spin takes to settle.[/*]
[*][b]Release spin strength[/b]: Lets you make the release spin weaker or stronger.[/*]
[/list]
